# Leaderboard Webserial Rubric

Use a 1-5 score in each category.

## Hook And Retention

- opening compulsion
- chapter-end propulsion
- immediate stakes clarity
- curiosity carry into the next unit

## Strategic Appeal

- protagonist agency
- protagonist competence or learning velocity
- visible tradeoffs
- opposition intelligence

## Mystery And Revelation

- question ladder clarity
- clue fairness
- meaning shift on reveal
- payoff inevitability

## Serial Addictiveness

- consequence density
- forward pressure
- escalating access to deeper layers
- binge continuity

## Power And Faction Depth

- advancement cost
- exploit space
- faction incentive clarity
- institutional pressure

## Prose And Readability

- clarity under complexity
- sentence momentum
- scene focus
- atmospheric function

## Score Meanings

- `5`: strong enough to ship
- `4`: good but still compressible or sharpenable
- `3`: workable but ordinary or leaky
- `2`: weak enough to damage retention
- `1`: failing category

## Weighted Priorities

If the target is leaderboard performance, weight categories in this order:

1. Hook And Retention
2. Serial Addictiveness
3. Strategic Appeal
4. Mystery And Revelation
5. Power And Faction Depth
6. Prose And Readability
