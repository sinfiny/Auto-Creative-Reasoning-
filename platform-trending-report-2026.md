## Methodology Notes

- **SpaceBattles:** Data from the automated weekly "Spacebattles Trending Stories and Quests" thread, which tracks Most Active Threads, Rising Stars (by reactions/day), Most New Watchers, and Most Popular (mid-stage). Updated every Monday. Fandom share percentages are provided by the thread maintainer.
- **Royal Road:** Scraped from the platform's Best Rated, Popular This Week, and Ongoing Fictions ranking pages, which reflect real-time follower counts, view counts, and update recency.
- **Webnovel:** Scraped from the fanfic category page, sorted by Popular. Ratings and chapter counts are from the platform's public listings.
- **QQ:** Requires authentication; data reflects search-indexed thread titles and known community patterns rather than real-time statistics.
- **Monthly breakdowns since Jan 2026:** SpaceBattles provides weekly snapshots; the April 13, 2026 data is the most recent available. Royal Road and Webnovel rankings are point-in-time and don't offer historical monthly archives publicly. For monthly trend analysis, the SB thread's 119 threadmarks (dating back to Jan 2024) are the best longitudinal data source.
