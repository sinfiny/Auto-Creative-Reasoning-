### Webnovel Platform Characteristics

- **Massive chapter counts** — 600–2,000+ chapters is normal for popular titles
- **Machine-translation quality** varies widely; original Chinese novels sit alongside English originals
- **System/LitRPG mechanics** are the default, not the exception
- **Harem/romance** is more openly tagged than on SB or RR
- **R18 content** is available but gated; tagged explicitly on titles like The Force of the North
- **Rating system** is 1–5 stars; lower average ratings than RR because of volume and translation quality
