# Source Notes

This skill combines the strongest reusable lessons from Mark Forsyth and Ward Farnsworth, as mapped in `David-Perell-writing-skills-proof.md`.

Primary devices to reach for:

- anaphora
- contrast and antithesis
- reversal and chiasmus
- parallel syntax
- deliberate sentence endings

Boundary rule:

- use rhetoric to sharpen meaning
- do not use rhetoric to disguise conceptual weakness

This is a line-level device skill, not a full prose-overhaul system.
