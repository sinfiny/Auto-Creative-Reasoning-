# Source Notes

This skill is mainly grounded in the Johann Hari interview research workflow summarized in `David-Perell-writing-skills-proof.md`.

Key reusable moves:

- define the reporting question before collecting more material
- hunt contradiction, significance, and lived detail
- ask questions that produce scenes, tradeoffs, and decisions
- synthesize toward meaning instead of stacking quotes

This is a reporting workflow skill. It should stay distinct from line editing and from generic "research everything" prompts.
