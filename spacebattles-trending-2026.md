- **Gamer/LitRPG** — Mech Otaku's Touch, Might as Well, Longevity in Naruto

## Trending Tropes

- **Worm crossover engine** — Worm is the default starting universe for crossovers (21% of all readers)
- **Power fantasy SI into military/command roles** — Imperial Governor, Mech pilot, BattleTech commander
- **Gacha/game systems** — Chaos Gacha, gamer mechanics, LitRPG progression
- **Crossover absurdism** — Naruto/The Muppets style mashups, Worm/Musical, Pokémon/Worm
- **Harem/romance in cultivation/xianxia** — Heroine's Master, House Husband
- **Post-GM Worm** — "I want to help [WORM/MCU][Post-GM]" continues trending
- **BattleTech renaissance** — multiple BattleTech fics simultaneously charting
