# Source Notes

This skill draws mainly from David Perell's online-writing workshop material and the shorter idea-finding episode summarized in `David-Perell-writing-skills-proof.md`.

Core idea-finding logic:

- start with something that does not make sense yet
- mine recurring notes, irritations, obsessions, and contradictions
- separate a broad topic from a sharper claim or angle
- prefer ideas that can support an essay, post, or thread, not just a clever sentence

Use this upstream of outlining and drafting. Once the user has a real angle, hand off to a structure or prose skill.
