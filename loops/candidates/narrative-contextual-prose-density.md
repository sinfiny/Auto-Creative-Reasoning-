# Candidate: narrative-contextual-prose-density

## Why It Might Help

This skill can improve the loop when drafts fail because scene detail is mismatched to scene function.

## Likely Benchmark Benefit

- catches over-described scenes that should be causal
- catches under-described scenes where identity or atmosphere is the actual job
- gives a more precise reason than "the prose is off"

## Why It Is Not Default Yet

It is useful, but not every fiction unit needs density tuning as a first-order benchmark lens.

## Promotion Trigger

Promote it if multiple loop runs show that density mismatches are a recurring hidden cause of weak scores.
