# Source Notes

This skill is primarily grounded in the Johnny Harris interview material summarized in `David-Perell-writing-skills-proof.md`.

Core reusable workflow:

- make the viewer promise explicit early
- structure the script around visual transitions and reveals, not just spoken explanation
- renew attention through paradox, surprise, and progressive clarification
- simplify complexity without draining the story out of it

Use this for video essays, explainers, and documentary-style scripts. It is not a generic storytelling or thumbnail ideation skill.
