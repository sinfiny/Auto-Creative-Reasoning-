# Research Notes

This folder holds supporting research, source-proof notes, and trend reports that inform the broader system but are not themselves loop contracts or reusable runtime skills.

Use this area for:

- source investigations that explain why a skill exists
- platform or market trend reports
- synthesis notes that may later become skills, benchmarks, or product decisions

Do not treat this folder as a raw scrape dump. If large or noisy captures are needed locally, distill them before commit.
