# Faction Power Consequence Engine

Use this skill when the story needs a power structure that produces strategy instead of empty spectacle.

## Quick Start

- Use this for cultivation ladders, occult pathways, ritual systems, relic economies, sect politics, church politics, guild politics, and hidden organizations.
- Pair with `$strategic-cast-intelligence-engine` when the question is how smart actors exploit the system.
- Pair with `$strategic-webserial-architecture` when the system needs to feed reveals and long-arc escalation.

## Operating Loop

1. Define the currencies of power: knowledge, resources, status, rituals, artifacts, bodies, time, reputation, or taboo access.
2. Define advancement gates and their costs. Every stage should demand sacrifice, risk, or dependency.
3. Define how organizations control access, information, legitimacy, or punishment.
4. Identify exploit space. Smart characters should be able to bend the system without breaking its logic.
5. Map second-order consequences: social suspicion, corruption, madness, debt, surveillance, enemies, and opportunity cost.
6. Make every power gain change future strategy instead of only increasing battle output.
7. Check who benefits if the system stays as it is, and who would destabilize it.

## Design Contract

```text

```

## Pressure Heuristics

- If advancement has no lasting cost, it will flatten suspense.
- If factions do not control something scarce, they will feel decorative.
- If every stage only means "bigger attack," the system is under-designed.
- If the protagonist can win without exposing new risk, the economy is too forgiving.

## Rules

- Do not solve tension by inventing a new tier too early.
- Do not make organizations omniscient unless that surveillance itself has a mechanism and a price.
- Do not hide all system logic. The reader should understand enough to anticipate danger.
- Prefer power with side effects over power with flavor text.
- Use original mechanics and institutions rather than reskinning famous systems from existing webnovels.
