# Source Notes

This skill is primarily derived from the Steven Pinker interview identified in `David-Perell-writing-skills-proof.md`.

Core editing lens:

- defeat abstraction that hides the real point
- restore reader context before examples or mechanisms
- replace vague nouns with concrete scenes, objects, or actions
- diagnose why a sentence is hard to follow before rewriting it

Use this for nonfiction explanation and expository prose. It is narrower than a general style-improvement skill.
