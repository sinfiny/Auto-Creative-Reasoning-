### Unique RR Signatures (vs. other platforms)

- **Progression Fantasy** is the dominant genre label — not just tagging, but core identity
- **Kingdom Building** appears in 4 of the top 10 Rising Stars
- **Cultivation/Wuxia** is massively popular — Sky Pride (#2 ongoing), Path to Transcendence, Cultivating Common Sense
- **LitRPG/GameLit** is the default, not the exception — system/stat progression is baseline
- **VRMMO/Isekai** is the standard framing device — New Life as a Max Level Archmage, The Runesmith, Chaotic Craftsman
- **Original fiction dominates fan fiction** — unlike SpaceBattles, the vast majority of RR's top stories are original worlds
- **Patreon economics** — almost every top fiction links to a Patreon with advance chapters (typically 10-25 ahead)
