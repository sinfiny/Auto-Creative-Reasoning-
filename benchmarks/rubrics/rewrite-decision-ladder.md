# Rewrite Decision Ladder

Use this to decide what level to rewrite after judging a draft.

## Prose Rewrite

Choose this when:

- the structure works
- the clues are fair
- the chapter has value
- the draft is simply muddy, flat, or overwritten

## Scene Rewrite

Choose this when:

- a confrontation lacks pressure
- a reveal lands weakly
- the motives are unclear within one section

## Chapter Rewrite

Choose this when:

- the hook is weak
- too little changes
- the chapter ends without a real gain, loss, or discovery

## Arc Rewrite

Choose this when:

- reveals are out of order
- escalation stalls across multiple chapters
- factions are not moving in a believable way

## Premise Or System Rewrite

Choose this when:

- the power system cannot generate hard choices
- the protagonist has no compelling strategy path
- the core promise is not strong enough to support obsession

## Rule

Always choose the highest-level real failure, even if a lower-level edit feels easier.
